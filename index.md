# Who am I?

Hi! I'm Tanay Tummalapalli. Some people call me TT.

Currently, I work as a Software Engineer at [Clarisights](https://clarisights.com/) on the Database team. At Clarisights, I've worked extensively on the Data Pipelines team and contributed to the DevOps team.

I worked on Apache Beam as part of Google Summer of Code in 2019.
I've previously worked at startups like [SocialCops](https://socialcops.com/), and [GigSync](http://gigsync.in/)(first engineer).
I majored in Computer Science from GGS Indraprastha University, New Delhi. I graduated in 2019.
I'm interested in Databases, Distributed Systems and Programming Languages.

I enjoy listening to music and discovering new music.
Sometimes, I also make music.You can find it on [my Soundcloud](https://soundcloud.com/bagaichi).
I used to write a blog called [The Groovy Train](https://thegroovytrain.wordpress.com/) about the Independent Music Scene in Delhi while studying at university.

I'm also interested in Philosophy, Art, Comedy, Films, and History.
